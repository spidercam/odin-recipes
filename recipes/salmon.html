Salmon

Description

This is a simple salmon recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 - Salmon fillets
  • 1tbsp Butter
  • Smoked paprika
  • Herbs de provence
  • Sea salt

Steps

  1. Warm a frying pan with the butter.
  2. Add the salmon fillets to the pan skin-side down.
  3. Season with smoked paprika, herbs de provence, and sea salt.
  4. Flip when you see the salmon halfway cooked.
  5. Remove the skin and crisp on the side with the fillets.
