Meatballs

Description

Great gluten-free meatball recipe. Minimal dairy with parmesan cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1-1/2 lb Veal Chop Meat
  • 1-1/2 lb Top Round or Sirloin Chop Meat
  • 1 Cup GF Bread Crumbs
  • 1 Cup Water
  • 3/4 Cup Parmesan Cheese
  • 5 - Cloves Garlic (Chopped Fine)
  • Parsley Chopped
  • 3 Eggs

Steps

  1. Combine all the dry ingredients in a mixing bowl.
  2. Add all the wet ingredients to the bowl and mix.
  3. Roll into individual meatballs (should make 35).
  4. Cook on a pan with some oil.
