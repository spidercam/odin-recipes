GF Eggs Benedict

Description

Great gluten-free eggs benedict recipe.

Ingredients

  • Canyon Bakehouse GF English Muffins
  • Canadian bacon
  • Hollandaise Sauce
  • Poached eggs
  • Fresh chives
    • 2 large egg yolks
    • 1 tbsp water
    • 1 tbsp lemon juice
    • 1/2 cup butter (8 tbsp)
    • 1/4 tsp salt
    • Pinch of ground white pepper
    • Pinch cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. To make the hollandaise grab a medium sized saucepan and whisk together the egg yolks, water, and lemon juice. Heat the pan on low heat, whisking constantly. Add the butter 1 tbsp at a time, whisking/melting thoroughly before adding more. Never stop whisking, that is the trick here! Once all of the butter is melted into the sauce turn off the heat and add the salt, pepper, and cayenne.
  2. Once the sauce is done, set it aside and poach the eggs. To poach the eggs use the egg poacher. Fill water to 1 inch and turn the heat to boil. Cover the poacher and ring dish. Keep heat at medium. Crack open eggs into small dishes. Once water is boiling, put butter in each container and drop eggs in. Boil for 5 minutes.
  3. Toast the english muffins and add the ham on top, then place a poached egg on top of that. Drizzle with the hollandaise sauce and sprinkle with chopped chives and serve warm.
